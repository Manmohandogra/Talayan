The Idea

Talayan is a study of change.

A performance traces the stages of artistic transformation — how artists evolve as they take in new ideas, cultures and ways of creating.

Each piece is a different phase in a journey toward something new. Rhythm is the common thread: a universal language that carries influences across borders and lets three great traditions speak to one another.

India

Classical Roots

The tabla and the cyclical grammar of tala — centuries of rhythmic craft as the anchoring voice.

The West

Jazz & Beyond

The drum kit, improvisation and the freedom of jazz — conversation, risk, and swing.

Africa

The Deep Pulse

Polyrhythm and groove — the ancestral heartbeat that binds it all together.

The Masterclass

Rhythm, without borders.

Built on the collaboration between drummer Siemy D and tabla player Manmohan Dogra, the Talayan masterclass explores rhythm as a universal language — weaving together influences from Africa, jazz and Western music, and Indian classical traditions.

Who it's for

  • Musicians of all levels — and all instruments, not only percussionists.
  • Anyone drawn to rhythm, creativity and cross-cultural discovery.
  • Players curious how different musical cultures connect, complement and inspire one another.

An interactive demonstration of musical ideas — not a lecture.

What you'll gain

  • A broader musical and cultural understanding.
  • Stronger rhythmical wellness and inner timing.
  • Practical tools you can take straight back to your own playing.
  • New ways of thinking about how cultures can play as one.
